What "Halal-Assured" Actually Means — And Why It's More Meaningful Than a Certificate

Halal-assured is an operating model of direct accountability. At Mister Wok, it means sourcing and kitchen handling standards maintained daily, not treated as periodic paperwork.

This article explains the distinction clearly for guests: halal certified and halal-assured are not identical terms, but both are about trust. Mister Wok communicates its exact position openly so customers can make informed choices.
